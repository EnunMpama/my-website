About Me

Where structure
meets strategy

Over the years, I have built a practice at the intersection of operations and communications — providing solutions that help founders and organisations move with clarity, intention, and scale. My work spans inbox and scheduling management, workflow design, process documentation, and strategic written communication — all engineered to position brands for sustainable impact.

At Niger & Nile LP, I led the redesign of the firm's case management and financial tracking systems using advanced Excel and Google Sheets techniques — streamlining workflows across 500+ cases and achieving 80% process automation. I also standardised internal and stakeholder communication channels, drafted contracts, and authored SOPs that created consistency and accountability across the firm.

At the Journal of Intersectional Social Justice, I supported editorial operations, content development, and literature research — organising the workflows that keep priorities, people, and processes running in sync. The through-line across every engagement is the same: I build systems that work even when you're not in the room.

"I don't just manage tasks — I design the environments in which great work becomes inevitable."
